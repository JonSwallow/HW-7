They drive for hours, though they have lived in the area all of their lives; nothing seems familiar.
There is a split in the road up ahead, should they turn left or right?
Choose the red button for left or the blue button for right.

Pushing the red button

Well my friend it seems you have chosen to turn left.
Congratulations!! Your choice means absolutely nothing.
Or does it....?

The subject of our story now turns their vehicle to the left.
After a couple of minutes they see a familiar house off to the side of the road.
Getting their bearings once again our hero or heroine whichever the case may be;
perhaps even our non binary subject of interest, finds their way back to where they started.
Opting to go the route they know, they end up being several hours late for work, and receive a mild repremand.
